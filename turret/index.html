Basic Controls

You can aim and fire the turret by clicking the buttons below. Use the live video feed on the right from the turret's webcam to see what you're aiming at. There is a small laser attached to the bottom of the turret to help you aim, as well. Keep in mind that it takes a little bit of time for the turret to get ready to fire again, and make sure to keep an eye on how much ammo you have left. You can also control the turret by using the following controls while in the applet:

W = UP
A = LEFT
S = DOWN
D = RIGHT
SPACE = FIRE
